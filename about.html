Why we built LeafLettr

English learners in Bangladesh often face a massive hurdle when trying to read literature or academic papers. The constant disruption of looking up words makes it nearly impossible to enjoy a book.

It is honestly "biroktikor" to search for words in the dictionary again and again. It breaks your concentration and kills the joy of reading.

That’s why we built LeafLettr. We wanted to create a precision tool where the meaning of a word is just a hover away. By providing instant Bengali translations within the document, we help you stay immersed in the story while building your vocabulary naturally.

Technical Notes & Warnings

Accuracy Disclaimer

We sincerely apologize if you encounter any inaccurate translations. While our AI attempts to provide the most relevant Bengali meaning, context can be tricky. We are constantly working to improve our database for better precision.

Usage Requirement

Please ensure you upload **text-based PDFs**. If your PDF is made of scanned images (where you cannot select text), our hover engine will not be able to identify the words.
